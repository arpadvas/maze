Welcome to the Maze!

The game objective is finding the way out of the maze. You can control your character by using the arrow keys.

The following actions can be done:

  • You can open items by pressing "O".
  • You can use toggle switches by pressing "T".
  • You can try to break items by using "B".
  • If you meet a Non-playing character you can try to speak with them by pressing "S".

During your adventure you might find items which will be showing on the top of the screen. You are automatically going to use them when needed.

Good luck to your challenge!
